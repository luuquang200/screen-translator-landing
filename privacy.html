Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

🔒 Short version: We only process the specific screen region you select. We never access your full screen, browsing history, or any other personal data. Only PRO users share their email address, used solely for license activation.

1. Who We Are

Screen Translator is a Chrome browser extension developed and maintained independently. For privacy-related questions, contact us at luungocquang2002@gmail.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide our service:

Data Who Why Stored?
Selected screen region (image) All users OCR text extraction and translation No — processed and discarded immediately
Device ID (randomly generated UUID) All users Track monthly free tier usage (20 captures/month) Yes — stored in our backend database
IP address All users Rate limiting and abuse prevention Yes — stored in our backend database
Email address PRO users only License key activation and customer support Yes — stored in our backend database
License key PRO users only Verify PRO subscription status Yes — stored in our backend database

3. What We Never Collect

  • Your full screen or anything outside the region you select
  • Your browsing history or visited URLs
  • Passwords or authentication credentials
  • Payment information (handled entirely by LemonSqueezy)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Mouse clicks, keystrokes, or user activity
  • Any data from pages you visit without triggering the extension

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Screen region image: Sent to our OCR provider (Azure Computer Vision) to extract text, then immediately discarded. Never stored or used for any other purpose.
  • Device ID: Used only to count your monthly free captures and reset them at the start of each month.
  • IP address: Used only for rate limiting to prevent abuse of our service.
  • Email address: Used only to verify your PRO license and provide customer support. Never used for marketing without explicit consent.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide our functionality:

Service Purpose Data Shared Privacy Policy

MyMemory / Google Translate Text translation Extracted text only Google Privacy

6. Data Retention

  • Screen region images: Never stored — discarded immediately after OCR processing
  • Device ID and request counts: Reset monthly, deleted upon request
  • IP address: Reset monthly, deleted upon request
  • PRO user email and license: Retained while subscription is active, deleted within 30 days of cancellation upon request

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • The third-party services listed above, solely to provide our service
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights

We will never sell your data to advertisers or data brokers.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Correct: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt out: Uninstall the extension to stop all data collection immediately

To exercise any of these rights, email us at luungocquang2002@gmail.com. We will respond within 7 days.

9. Children's Privacy

Screen Translator is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data including:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS encryption
  • API key authentication on all backend endpoints
  • Row Level Security (RLS) enabled on our database
  • Rate limiting to prevent unauthorized access

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

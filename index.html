FAQ

Common questions

Does it work on images and videos?

Yes! Unlike most translation tools that only work on selectable text, Screen Translator uses OCR to extract text from any visual content — images, video frames, games, and more.

What languages are supported?

We support 50+ languages including English, Vietnamese, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, French, German, Spanish, Portuguese, and many more.

Is my data private?

Only the specific screen region you select is sent for OCR processing. Your full screen, browsing history, and other data are never accessed or stored.

How does the PRO license key work?

After payment, you receive a license key via email. Enter it once in the extension settings to activate PRO on any device. Your key works across reinstalls and devices.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your PRO subscription at any time from the LemonSqueezy customer portal. No questions asked.

What if I reach my free limit?

Your limit resets automatically every month. Or upgrade to PRO for unlimited captures.
